About NCCU MIS

National Chengchi University (NCCU) Department of Management of Information System is a leading academic institution in Taiwan, renowned for its excellence in information management education and research. Established as part of National Chengchi University, the department has a rich history and has played a pivotal role in shaping the field of information management in Taiwan.

The Department of Management of Information System at NCCU offers a comprehensive and interdisciplinary curriculum that encompasses areas such as data science, information systems, and digital innovation. Students in the program gain a solid foundation in theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for diverse careers in the rapidly evolving information technology industry.

Faculty members at the Department of Management of Information System are experts in their respective fields, actively engaged in cutting-edge research that contributes to advancements in information system management. The department is committed to fostering a dynamic learning environment, encouraging students to explore innovative solutions to real-world challenges and promoting a global perspective in the field of information system management.
